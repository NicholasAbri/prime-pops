About PRIME POPS

Made fresh, packed with care.

Prime Pops was created with one goal: to make premium popcorn affordable without compromising quality.

Every batch is freshly made after your preorder is confirmed and carefully packed before scheduled delivery, so your popcorn arrives crisp, warm, and ready for the moment.

Delivery Areas

Delivery is available within selected areas in Ghana. Send your location on WhatsApp to confirm availability and the delivery fee before payment.

Allergy Notice

FAQ

Common questions

How do I place an order?

Select your preferred pack, choose your flavor, adjust the quantity, then click the WhatsApp preorder button to send your order.

When is my popcorn prepared?

Every order is freshly made after your preorder is confirmed to ensure maximum freshness and quality.

Do you deliver?

Yes. Delivery is available within selected areas. Delivery fees depend on your location.

Can I preorder for birthdays, church programs, school events, or meetings?

Yes. We accept bulk preorders for birthdays, church programs, schools, offices, movie nights, and other special events.

What flavors are available?

Available flavors are displayed in the Products section. More flavors will be added over time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Mobile Money (MoMo). Payment instructions will be shared after your preorder has been confirmed on WhatsApp.

How much notice should I give for bulk orders?

Please place bulk orders 2–3 days in advance so we can prepare your popcorn fresh and have it ready on schedule.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Please contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible. Changes or cancellations may not be possible once preparation has started.

Do your flavors contain allergens?

Some flavors may contain milk and are prepared in the same environment. If you have allergies or dietary concerns, please let us know before placing your order.

When is my preorder confirmed?

Your preorder is confirmed only after PRIME POPS replies on WhatsApp and payment or delivery arrangements have been agreed.
